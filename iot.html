Internet of Things (IoT) Fundamentals


Certificate is avilable for this course


Welcome to the Internet of Things (IoT) Fundamentals course! In this course, you'll explore the exciting world of IoT and gain a solid understanding of its key concepts and applications.


Overview


This course provides a comprehensive introduction to IoT, covering topics such as:


  • Understanding the IoT ecosystem and architecture
  • Sensor technologies and data acquisition
  • IoT communication protocols and networks
  • IoT security and privacy considerations


Assessments

Regular assessments will test your knowledge of IoT concepts and your ability to apply them to real-world scenarios. These assessments are designed to encourage critical thinking and problem-solving skills in the context of IoT technology.


Quizzes

Regular quizzes will help you assess your progress and ensure you're grasping the core concepts. These quizzes are designed to be engaging and informative, providing you with instant feedback on your performance.


Hands-On Projects

Throughout the course, you'll engage in practical projects that allow you to work with real IoT devices and technologies. These projects will help you develop the skills needed to create and deploy your own IoT solutions.
